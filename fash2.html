How to Know Your Style!

If you made it to this page,a big shout-out to you! There's nothing more beautiful than finding yourself and enhancing yourself. There are some ways by which you can discover your style and I'm here to tell you;

1. Finding the clothes you're comfortable in!

Believe me if I tell you that wearing clothes that are not your style show, in fact,glare at the people who look at you. So the first step is knowing what you feel most comfortable wearing.Clothes you know that if you wear,you'd never feel out of your skin or suffocated in! You must be able to feel confident wearing these clothes,feel like you could take on the world! Once you have found these clothes or clothing accessories,then we are good to go!

2. Finding clothes that compliment your body shape

Out of these comfort clothes you have found,you need to filter further to clothes for your body. As we all know,there are different body shapes from hourglass( figure 8) to pear, apple etc;there are some clothes that can fit some body shapes and make others look not that great. So you need to know your body shape and the clothes that will fit you and present you well.
